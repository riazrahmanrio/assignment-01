Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

New Bangladesh is innovative. Researchers try to invent new things.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh includes efforts to reduce environmental impact, promote social responsibility, and ensure economic prosperity.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh infrastructure is developing rapidly, with new projects and investments helping to improve connectivity and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh refers to the process of giving people the tools to make decisions, access resources, and solve problems.

The Vision of future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future. This will be better for our environment.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
